Welcome to Food World

The right place to have good taste!

Restaurants "FOOD WORD" is a national, not-for-profit association representing FOOD WORD's diverse and dynamic restaurant and foodservice industry. With more than one hundreds employees and One thousand customers a day, the restaurant industry is the number one source of first jobs for young people. We help build neighbourhoods, drive tourism. Restaurants FOOD WORD members comprise 30,000 businesses in every segment of the industry, including restaurants, caterers, institutions and their suppliers. We work on their behalf with all levels of government in support of a strong restaurant industry that contributes jobs, investment, and a wide range of culinary choices to communities across the country. Restaurants Canada is at the forefront of critical issues such as food safety, nutrition information and allergy awareness, through specialized training programs, information and resources. We also offer a suite of industry research, information, events, programs and discounts that keep our members informed and competitive. Founded in 1995, we were first known as the Food zone Restaurant Association, then the Food zone Restaurant and Foodservices Association. We celebrated our 24th anniversary by unveiling a new logo, name and tagline: Food World, The voice of foodservice. Our head office is in Ujjain.
